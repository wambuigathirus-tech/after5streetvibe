About After 5 Street Vibe

More than an event. It’s a movement.

Overview

After 5 Street Vibe is a monthly street experience in Nakuru City that brings youth, creatives, entrepreneurs, music lovers, and brands together in an energetic urban setting. It provides local talent a platform and supports music, art, fashion, dance, emceeing, content creation, and community networking.

Mission

To build a powerful street culture movement that empowers creatives, connects communities, and gives young people a consistent platform to express talent and grow.

Vision

To become the biggest street culture experience in the Rift Valley, expanding to other major towns in Kenya.

Our Objectives

What we want to achieve

Promote local talent

Provide a visible platform for upcoming Nakuru artists to showcase talent and gain exposure.

Foster community engagement

Create a cultural hub where the community can appreciate and celebrate local artistry.

Encourage collaboration

Facilitate interactions between emerging artists and established creatives, opening doors for mentorship and partnerships.

Enhance vibrancy

Offer diverse themed shows that reflect Nakuru’s artistic heritage and creative ecosystem.
